Our Features

CleverBooks provides real-time updates on inventory levels across multiple locations. This feature ensures that businesses always have accurate data on stock availability, which helps in making informed decisions and reducing the risk of stockouts or overstocking.

CleverBooks offers advanced reporting and analytics tools that provide insights into inventory performance, sales trends, and supplier efficiency. These reports can be customized and scheduled for automatic delivery to key stakeholders

CleverBooks seamlessly integrates with popular eCommerce platforms like Shopify, WooCommerce, and Amazon. This integration ensures that inventory levels are automatically updated across all sales channels, providing a unified view of inventory and preventing overselling.
